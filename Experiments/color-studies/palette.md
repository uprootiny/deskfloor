## Experiments to Try

### Warm Dark
Replace the cool blue-black with a warmer dark:
- Background: rgb(0.10, 0.09, 0.08)
- Cards: rgb(0.14, 0.12, 0.11)
- Evokes: wood, paper, warmth

### High Contrast
For accessibility and outdoor use:
- Pure black background
- White text at 90%+ for all readable content
- Saturated accent colors
- Minimum 4.5:1 contrast ratio everywhere
